About the Project

Built for the IBM SkillsBuild

A sequential AI agent that reasons through a company the way an equity analyst does — one verified step at a time.

Problem Statement

Manual investment research is slow and fragmented. Analysts jump between filings, ratio spreadsheets, news feeds, and competitor data just to answer one question: is this a good investment? The process is repetitive, hard to standardize, and easy to get wrong under time pressure.

Objectives

  • Automate end-to-end financial data collection and validation
  • Chain sequential AI tasks that mirror a real analyst workflow
  • Score risk and sentiment alongside the fundamentals
  • Output a single, explainable BUY / HOLD / SELL call

IBM Orchestrate

Coordinates and sequences every task the agent performs, so each step's output feeds cleanly into the next.

IBM Granite

Enterprise-grade foundation models power the reasoning behind every KPI read, risk flag, and recommendation.

Sequential AI

A nine-step chained pipeline, not a single black-box prediction — every output can be traced back to a step.

How It Works

Nine steps, one continuous chain

Every step's output becomes the next step's input — that sequence is the whole point.

  1. 01

    Collect Financial Data

    The agent gathers filings, statements, and live market data for the selected company.

  2. 02

    Validate Information

    Cross-checks every data point for accuracy and consistency before analysis begins.

  3. 03

    Extract KPIs

    Identifies the core performance indicators that drive the investment decision.

  4. 04

    Calculate Financial Ratios

    Computes PE, ROE, debt, and profitability ratios automatically.

  5. 05

    Compare Competitors

    Benchmarks the company against its closest industry rivals.

  6. 06

    Analyze Market Sentiment

    Gauges investor and media sentiment from recent market activity.

  7. 07

    Detect Risks

    Surfaces financial, operational, and market risk signals worth flagging.

  8. 08

    Generate AI Recommendation

    Synthesizes every prior step into a single BUY / HOLD / SELL call.

  9. 09

    Generate Investment Report

    Compiles a structured, shareable report of the complete analysis.
